## Honest gaps (not surprises)

- 114 of 171 discovered episodes are still IP-blocked from YouTube's transcript API. Ingestion pipeline is checked-in and idempotent — runs overnight when the block lifts. None of this is hidden.
- The synthesis is deterministic, not real-time. A new episode triggers a 5-minute rebuild, not a live re-extraction.
- The shipped artefact is macOS-native (Tauri + Rust + SQLite). Linux/Windows builds work too but aren't the primary surface.
